ABOUT LYVARIA COMPANY

LYVARIA is headquartered in Ecuador. This company is dedicated to the development of tools with 3D technology, ranging since the building of plans to the design of finished spaces as hotels, restaurants, museums, events centers, bars, amusement parks, malls, among other places considered part of tourism sector, helping in this way its clients and partners to promote their businesses and therefore promote tourism.

Objects that are part of one of the aforementioned tourist attractions, such as works of art in museums, are also designed. We have reliable channels to facilitate payments to your customers (credit cards and online payment platforms).

MISSION

At Lyvaria Company, our mission is to provide 3D technology solutions to the tourism sector.

VISION

To be a recognized company in providing promotion solutions through 3D technology to the tourism and hotel sector.

SOFREST
